Quickly becoming a destination for Milwaukee’s creative community, Riverworks Business Improvement District is taking the lead as we invite national perspectives and local experts to spend a week connecting the relationship of place, space, and progress.

Riverworks has a number of spaces ready for transformation - possibilities are present for both the large scale manufacturer and the upstart shopkeep. With a new economy revealing itself, we will focus on entrepreneurship, community sustainability, and the reawakening of urban land banks. Join us for the week or just one event. Below you will find more information on each of the day's activities, space is limited so be sure to register.
